Neyveli Lignite Corporation

The Neyveli Lignite Corporation (NLC) is one of India's largest lignite mining and power generation companies. Located in Tamil Nadu, it plays a vital role in the country's energy sector while also emphasizing sustainable practices. The site incorporates extensive land reclamation and environmental restoration initiatives, ensuring the ecosystem's revival after mining activities.

NLC's efforts include afforestation projects, water management systems, and the use of bio-fertilizers to restore soil quality. These measures are part of its commitment to transforming mining-affected areas into productive and ecologically balanced landscapes.

Drought-Resistant Species

Drought-resistant species such as Bamboo, Jatropha, and Prosopis juliflora are planted to improve resilience and maintain ecological balance in arid and semi-arid conditions.

Ideal Planting Density

An optimal planting density of 70-80% coverage is being adopted to maximize ecological benefits and ensure sustainable land restoration.
